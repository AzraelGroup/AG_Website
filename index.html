About Tim McKenna

Tim McKenna is a Design Director and Strategist who works with organizations to launch innovative products and services. With 15 years of building and leading multi-disciplinary design teams in technology, government, education, and healthcare, Tim knows how to make great products that sell. It starts with focusing on your customer and building solutions that connect and communicate with their audience.

Tim has elevated design teams for national and global organizations. He has spoken at industry events and conferences, including AIGA, GovTech Summit, Interaction Week, OFFSET, and Abstractions. His own articles have been featured in Healthcare IT News, A List Apart, Smashing Magazine, and UX Collective. In addition to his voluminous user experience and product design experience, Tim is a credentialed expert in project management and digital accessibility from the Project Management Institute and the International Association of Accessibility Professionals, respectively.

Tim holds a BFA in design from Hussian College in Philadelphia, PA, an AAS in web development from HACC in Harrisburg, PA, as well as additional programs on interaction design and organizational leadership from UC San Diego and Northwestern University.
